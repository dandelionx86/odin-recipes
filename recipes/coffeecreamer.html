Coffee Creamer - Homemade Hack

Elevate your morning coffee with this delicious homemade creamer recipe.

This is what you'll need:

  • 1 c. sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 c. whole milk
  • 1/4 tsp. almond, vanilla, or peppermint extract (go easy with the extract)
  • Whisk
  • Mixing bowl
  • Airtight container, to store

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together condensed milk, whole milk, and flavored extract of your choice.
  2. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 1 week. Enjoy!
